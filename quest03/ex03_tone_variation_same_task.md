# Objective: Control tone and style with explicit instructions.

    1. Choose one task.

        "Write an email as a CEO of a high end restaurant, laying of a waitress"

    2. Write two prompts:

        One for a friendly tone

            "Write an email as a CEO of a high end restaurant (The Elixir), laying of a waitress (Sandra). Make her understand why so she doesn't have any hard feelings. (medium temperature)"

                Dear Sandra,

                I want to personally thank you for the dedication and warmth you’ve brought to The Elixir. Your presence has been valued, and this decision was not made lightly.

                As you know, The Elixir operates at a very high standard of precision, consistency, and guest experience. After careful review, we’ve determined that we need to make adjustments within the service team to better align with the pace and expectations required at this level of dining. This decision is about maintaining our brand standards rather than a reflection of your character or potential.

                I genuinely appreciate the effort you’ve shown during your time with us. I would be happy to provide a positive reference and support you as you transition to your next opportunity. I believe you have strengths that will thrive in the right environment.

                Thank you again for your contribution to The Elixir, and I wish you every success moving forward.

                Warm regards,
                [Your Name]
                Chief Executive Officer
                The Elixir

        One for a professional tone

            "Write an email as a CEO of a high end restaurant (The Elixir), laying of a waitress (Sandra), keep it simple and direct. (low temperature)"


                Dear Sandra,

                This letter is to inform you that your employment with The Elixir will be ending effective immediately.

                As a high-end establishment, we maintain strict service standards and performance expectations. After reviewing recent evaluations, we have determined that your performance does not consistently meet the level required for our dining experience.

                This decision is final. You will receive information regarding your final pay and any remaining formalities from management.

                We appreciate the time you spent with us and wish you the best in your future endeavors.

                Sincerely,
                [Your Name]
                Chief Executive Officer
                The Elixir



    3. Compare the outputs and note how the tone changes while the content stays consistent.

        Both emails communicate the same core message: Sandra is being laid off because her performance does not meet The Elixir’s service standards. The main information in both remains consistent, including appreciation for her time and confirmation of the decision. However, the tone differs noticeably.
        
        The friendly toned email is more empathetic and considerate in its wording. It explains the reason in a softer way and attempts to reduce potential hard feelings. In contrast, the professionally toned email is more direct, concise, and emotionally neutral, focusing strictly on the decision and its finality.
